The interface uses a simple design. Click the icon in the bottom system tray once to open the chat window. 

- You type your questions into the box at the bottom.
- Press the "Enter" key on your keyboard to send your message.
- The AI will provide a response in the main window.
- You can scroll up and down to see your past questions.
- Click outside the window to hide it. The app remains active in the background.

The application connects to the ChatGPT service to process your requests. It does not store your personal files. It only sends the text you type in the box to the server. You should avoid entering sensitive information like your home address, financial data, or passwords. Treat this chat like a public conversation.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

Most users solve issues by trying these steps:

**The app does not launch**
Check if you have an active internet connection. The app requires data to function. Ensure that your firewall does not block the application. 

**The chat window does not appear**
Look at the bottom right corner of your screen. Click the small arrow to see hidden icons. If you see the app icon, click it. You may need to click the tray icon one more time to force the window to reappear.

**The app runs slowly**
Close other programs that use too much memory. A high number of open tabs in your web browser can slow down other applications. Restarting your computer often clears up temporary performance issues.
